The Laws of the Court

Summary of the Court

Each Servant occupies a Standing within the King's Court. Each Standing only has room for one Servant. If someone is moved forward, then someone else is pushed further away from the King.

The four Standings closest to the King (Love, Friend, Guide, and Heir) are his Inner Circle.

A Servant's Agenda is determined by their Authority. Each month, a member of the King's Inner Circle will achieve their Agenda, changing Isha in the process.

If you're in the Inner Circle, you may use your Agenda as well, to Impact Isha. If you're in the King's Inner Circle, you must Spend Time each month to maintain your Standing.

You may Spend Time to move any Servant to an adjacent Standing, or if someone is at the Risky Standings (Harlot, Traitor, Worthless, Disgrace), to remove them from the Court.

You can also Spend Time to spy on Servants, and learn how to change their Allegiance or Authority.

Each month, consult the Stars in alignment with the King's Sun, Moon, and Rising signs.

  • Mercury: Who the King pushes forward or pushes away.
  • Venus: Which member of the Inner Circle's Agenda occurs.
  • Mars: The King's mood, lust, and spite.
  • Jupiter: Who joins the Court and what Allegiances hold sway.
  • Saturn: The state of violence within the Court.
  • The Sun: The King's regal performance.
