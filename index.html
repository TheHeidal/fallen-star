Old Master:

He was the last of an older kind of hero, a chivalrous knight who raised you on stories of adventure and codes of honor. He was killed by a wild boar during a hunting expedition, a great tragedy that all of Isha mourned.

Age:

37 score

Changes of magic:

  1. You can move as fast as falling rain, as fast as a hawk, as fast as a battlecry.
  2. You are as tall and broad as three men put together, and have the hunger of an army.
  3. An extra pair of arms burst from your shoulders, strong enough to lift mountains.
  4. Your face contorts to resemble a war-mask, with fearsome eyes and tusks jutting from your lips.
  5. You can smell blood better than a hound, and you can smell in someone's sweat their fear and hate.
  6. A flaming ring burns above your head, which shines brighter in your anger and fury.
  7. Your blood is blue, and scalding for any who touch it.
  8. Armor grows from your skin, scales and plate etched with tales of your exploits.
  9. Multiple pairs of iron wings extend from your back, sharp enough to cut skin and strong enough to carry you through the air.
  10. When you rage your breath transforms into flames, and you may breathe fire the way a dragon might.
  11. A long barbed tail juts from your back, prehensile and dripping poison.
  12. Your teeth are sharpened to points and your tongue lolls from your mouth like a dog.
  13. You crave the taste of human flesh.

Manhood:

No one dares speculate on your manhood. You would kill the next person to make such a claim in front of you.

Physical Appearance:

A dragon with scales the color of dried blood. A single sigil burns in the center of each one.

Pact Fragment:

  • When casting a spell directly against an unwilling foe, or when casting any spell during Combat (as discussed during Part IV), consult an additional die.
  • Interpret the presence of "⊖" (Salt) as "♂" (Mars).
  • Murder any mortal who stands before you with a single strike of your Pact-Fragment, faster than they can react and with such certainty that none may dispute their death.

Humble Magics:

  • Perform a daring feat of physical athleticism which dazzles others' senses, leap into the air, or lift heavy stones which no one else could lift.
  • Roar with a thunderous voice, or carry your voice across a battlefield, or act with the sounds of drums and horns at your steps, to motivate your troops or dismay others.
  • Place both hands upon someone's shoulders, and in an instant divine whether they are an eligible heir for the bloodline of Isha's royalty.
